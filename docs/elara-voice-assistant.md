# Elara Voice Assistant Guide

## Overview
Elara is your advanced AI-powered admin assistant with natural, human-like voice capabilities. She can help you with virtually ANYTHING related to course management and educational administration through natural conversation. Speak to her like you would to a skilled colleague!

## Getting Started

### Accessing Voice Features
1. Navigate to **Admin > AI Assistant**
2. Click the **"Show Voice"** button in the top-right corner
3. The voice controls panel will appear with microphone and speaker controls

### Browser Compatibility
- **Chrome/Edge**: Full voice support (recommended)
- **Firefox**: Limited support
- **Safari**: Limited support
- **Mobile browsers**: Varies by device

## Best Practices

### For Best Voice Recognition
1. **Speak naturally** - no need for special phrasing or keywords
2. **Use a quiet environment** to reduce background noise
3. **Allow microphone permissions** when prompted by your browser
4. **Use Chrome or Edge** for the best natural voices and performance
5. **Wait briefly** after Elara finishes speaking before your next request

### For Effective Communication
1. **Be conversational** - talk like you're chatting with a colleague
2. **Be specific** when you can, but Elara understands general requests too
3. **Ask follow-up questions** - Elara maintains context
4. **Don't worry about exact wording** - natural language works best
5. **Check the transcript** if you want to verify what was heard

## Troubleshooting

### Voice Not Working
- **Check browser permissions**: Ensure microphone access is allowed
- **Try a different browser**: Chrome/Edge work best
- **Check system audio**: Ensure microphone is working in other apps
- **Refresh the page**: Sometimes helps with permission issues

### Commands Not Recognized
- **Speak more clearly**: Enunciate each word
- **Use simpler phrases**: Try shorter, more direct commands
- **Check the transcript**: See if your words were captured correctly
- **Try typing instead**: Use the regular form inputs as backup

### Audio Feedback Issues
- **Check system volume**: Ensure speakers/headphones are working
- **Browser audio settings**: Check if audio is muted in browser
- **Try different browser**: Some browsers have audio limitations
